Frequently Asked Questions – Contract Law

What happens if a contract is breached?

If a party fails to fulfil their contractual obligations, the innocent party may claim damages, demand specific performance or cancel the contract depending on the circumstances. Legal advice ensures the correct remedy is pursued.

Can a verbal contract be enforced?

Yes, verbal agreements can be legally binding in many instances. However, proving the terms of a verbal contract can be difficult, which is why written agreements are always recommended.

Do I need a lawyer to draft contracts?

While not legally required, professional drafting ensures that the agreement is clear, enforceable and protects your interests, reducing the risk of future disputes.
