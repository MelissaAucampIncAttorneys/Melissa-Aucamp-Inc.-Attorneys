Family law deals with legal matters arising from personal relationships and family structures. These matters often involve sensitive issues and require a professional and compassionate approach to resolving disputes while protecting the rights of all parties involved.

Divorce Proceedings

Divorce can be emotionally and legally complex. Our firm assists clients in navigating divorce proceedings, ensuring that issues relating to division of assets, maintenance and parental responsibilities are addressed effectively.

Parental Rights & Responsibilities

South African law prioritises the best interests of the child when determining parental rights and responsibilities. We assist clients with matters involving care, contact and guardianship arrangements.

Protection Orders & Harassment Matters

Where individuals experience harassment or domestic conflict, legal protection may be necessary. Our firm assists clients with obtaining protection orders and resolving related disputes.

Frequently Asked Questions – Family Law

How long does a divorce take?

The duration depends on whether the divorce is contested or uncontested. Uncontested divorces may be finalised within a few weeks, while contested matters can take several months or longer.

Who gets custody of the child?

The court determines parental responsibilities and rights based on the best interests of the child, considering factors such as stability, care and the child's wellbeing.

Can maintenance be enforced?

Yes. If a party fails to pay maintenance, legal steps can be taken to enforce the order, including garnishee orders or other enforcement mechanisms.
