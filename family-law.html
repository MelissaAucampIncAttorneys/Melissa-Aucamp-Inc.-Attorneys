Family Law

At Melissa Aucamp Inc. Attorneys, we provide clear advice and practical solutions while prioritising the best interests of our clients and any children involved.

Our Family Law Services Include:

  • Maintenance disputes
  • Parenting plans and child custody
  • Domestic violence and harassment matters
  • Protection orders
