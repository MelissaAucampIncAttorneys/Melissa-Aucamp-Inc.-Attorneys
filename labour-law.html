Labour Law

Labour law governs the legal relationship between employers and employees and ensures fairness, compliance, and lawful conduct in the workplace. It regulates employment contracts, workplace discipline, dismissals, and dispute resolution mechanisms such as the CCMA.

At Melissa Aucamp Inc. Attorneys, we assist both employers and employees with clear legal guidance, strategic representation, and practical solutions aimed at resolving workplace disputes efficiently.

Our Labour Law Services Include:

  • Drafting and reviewing employment contracts
  • Disciplinary hearings and internal procedures
  • CCMA conciliations, arbitrations and hearings
  • Unfair dismissal and unfair labour practice disputes
  • Retrenchments and workplace misconduct matters
