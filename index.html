Our Practice Areas

Labour Law

Labour law regulates the relationship between employers and employees and ensures fairness, compliance, and accountability in the workplace. It governs rights, duties, and dispute resolution mechanisms in employment matters.

  • Drafting and reviewing employment contracts
  • Disciplinary hearings and internal processes
  • CCMA disputes, conciliations and arbitrations
  • Unfair dismissal and unfair labour practice claims
  • Retrenchments and workplace misconduct matters

Family Law

Family law deals with legal issues arising from family relationships and personal circumstances. These matters often require sensitivity, discretion, and firm legal guidance.

  • Divorce proceedings
  • Maintenance disputes
  • Parenting plans and child custody
  • Domestic violence and harassment matters
  • Protection orders

Contract Law

Contract law governs legally binding agreements and ensures that parties understand and comply with their obligations. Properly drafted contracts reduce disputes and protect legal rights.

  • Drafting and reviewing contracts
  • Breach of contract disputes
  • Lease and service agreements
  • Settlement agreements
  • Contract enforcement and cancellation

Commercial Law

Commercial law regulates business activities and commercial relationships. We assist businesses with legal compliance, dispute resolution, and strategic legal advice.

  • Business and commercial agreements
  • Debt collection and recovery
  • Commercial litigation
  • Shareholder and partnership disputes

Property Law

Property law concerns rights and obligations relating to immovable property, including residential and commercial properties.

  • Rental and lease disputes
  • Evictions
  • Landlord and tenant matters
  • Municipal and electricity disputes

Public Law

Public law governs the relationship between individuals and the state, ensuring lawful and fair administrative action.

  • Administrative law matters
  • Review of government decisions
  • Regulatory and constitutional issues

All Other Civil Law Matters

If your legal matter does not fall within a specific category, we are able to assess the matter and advise on the most appropriate legal course of action.
